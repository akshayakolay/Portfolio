How to multiply numbers

Here you will find the instructions to perform subtraction on the Calculator app.

To successfully perform subtraction operation, you must know about the concept of subtraction.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the multiply (x) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.
The multiplication result is displayed on the screen.
You want to multiply 2 and 5. The equation will look like 5x2=10. You enter 5. Press (x). Enter 2. Press (=)

The result 10 appears on the screen.
