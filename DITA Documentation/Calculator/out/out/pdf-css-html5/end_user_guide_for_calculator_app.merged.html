What is the addition of numbers

In this section, you will learn about the concept of addition.

To successfully perform the task of adding the numbers, you must know what is addition. The term "Addition" represents a mathematical operation. Addition is a process, an action that you perform using numbers. Addition allows you to find the sum of the numbers.

You use the (+) sign to perform the operation in the app.

Addition is the arithematic operation to produce a sum of two or more numbers.

How to add numbers

Here you will find the instructions to perform addition on the Calculator app.

To successfully perform addition operation, you must know about the concept of addition.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the add (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.
The addition result is displayed on the screen.
You want to add 2 and 5. The equation will look like 2+5=7. You enter 2. Press (+). Enter 5. Press (=)

The result 7 appears on the screen.
